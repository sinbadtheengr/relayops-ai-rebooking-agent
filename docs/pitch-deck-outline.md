# Pitch Deck Outline

1. Title: RelayOps AI Rebooking Agent
2. Problem: Small businesses lose repeat revenue when customers do not rebook.
3. Customer: Salons, clinics, wellness studios, and home-service SMBs with booking tools but no automation team.
4. Existing Pain: Manual calls, inconsistent staff follow-up, no prioritization, no ROI view.
5. Product: An AI employee in Slack that finds overdue customers, ranks opportunities, drafts outreach, and logs action.
6. Demo: Daily scan, VIP filter, 90-day query, personalized draft, contacted action.
7. AI Architecture: GPT tool calling over verified CRM/booking facts, with RAG-ready data access.
8. Business Value: Recover missed bookings, protect retention, give owners a measurable morning workflow.
9. Market Entry: Facial boutiques and salons first, then dental, massage, physio, home services.
10. Pricing: $99-$299 per location per month plus optional integration setup.
11. Production Roadmap: Booking connectors, campaign analytics, automated follow-up queues, multi-location reporting.
12. Why Now: Slack is becoming the operational surface for AI employees.
13. Ask: Pilot partners and integration access after the hackathon.
